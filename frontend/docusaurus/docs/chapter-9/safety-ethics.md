# Safety & Ethics

## Introduction

As humanoid robots become more integrated into human environments, ensuring their safety and ethical behavior becomes paramount. This chapter explores the critical safety and ethical considerations in humanoid robotics.

## Safety in Humanoid Robotics

### Physical Safety
- **Collision avoidance**: Preventing harmful contact
- **Force limitation**: Controlling interaction forces
- **Emergency stopping**: Rapid response to dangerous situations
- **Safe motion planning**: Avoiding unsafe trajectories

### System Safety
- **Fault tolerance**: Handling component failures
- **Graceful degradation**: Maintaining safe operation
- **Redundancy**: Backup systems for critical functions
- **Error recovery**: Automatic fault handling

## Risk Assessment

### Hazard Identification
- **Physical hazards**: Collision, crushing, cutting
- **Environmental hazards**: Fire, electric shock, radiation
- **Operational hazards**: Unexpected behavior, failure modes
- **Human factors**: Misuse, misunderstanding, interaction errors

### Risk Mitigation
- **Inherent safety**: Designing out hazards
- **Safeguarding**: Protective measures
- **Information for use**: Warnings and instructions
- **Emergency measures**: Response procedures
