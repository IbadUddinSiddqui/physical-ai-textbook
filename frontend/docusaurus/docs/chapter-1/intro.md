## What is Physical AI?

<div class="textbook-section">
  <p><strong>Physical AI</strong> represents the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems. Unlike traditional AI that operates primarily in digital spaces, Physical AI deals with intelligent systems that interact with the real world through sensors and actuators.</p>
</div>

## The Embodiment Principle

<div class="key-concept">
  <h4>ğŸ’¡ Core Concept</h4>
  <p>The core concept behind Physical AI is that intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its environment. This challenges the classical view of AI as purely computational, suggesting instead that the body and its interactions with the world play a crucial role in intelligent behavior.</p>
</div>
